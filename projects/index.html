Deities of Duat / DEITIES Project

"DEITIES Project" is my ongoing personal project featuring design interpretations of the Ancient Egyptian gods, as well as other creatures and aspects adapted from the mythology.

Several characters will also be featured in my upcoming visual story titled Deities of Duat, which is developing in tandem to the larger project. Additional development and artwork may be found within my other portfolio pages and on the DEITIES Project Blog.
